About the Project

📌 Problem Statement

Retail investors and traders in India face three major challenges — Market Volatility, Data Complexity, and Decision-Making Bias. Most retail investors lack access to professional-grade analytical tools and rely on intuition or unverified tips — leading to poor investment decisions and financial losses.

💡 Our Solution

StockSense is a web-based stock trend prediction dashboard that uses Exponential Moving Average (EMA-12 / EMA-26) crossover analysis to predict whether an NSE-listed stock is heading into an UPTREND ▲ or DOWNTREND ▼ — providing data-driven, bias-free signals instantly.

⚙ How It Works
01
Select Stock
Choose any NSE-listed stock. Live OHLCV data is fetched from Yahoo Finance.
02
Compute EMA
EMA-12 (fast) and EMA-26 (slow) are calculated from real historical closing prices.
03
Detect Signal
Golden Cross (EMA-12 > EMA-26) = BUY. Death Cross = SELL. Instant crossover detection.
04
View Forecast
Day-wise price forecast up to 30 days with confidence score and downloadable report.

📋 Step-by-Step Guide
01
🔑 Login to StockSense

Click the Login or Start Predicting button on the homepage. Enter your email and password to sign in. New users can create a free account using the Sign Up tab.

💡 You can also sign in quickly using Google or GitHub.
02
📌 Select a Stock

On the dashboard, use the Select Stock dropdown to choose any NSE-listed Indian stock — such as RELIANCE, TCS, INFY, HDFC Bank, and more. Live data will load automatically from Yahoo Finance.

💡 Wait 2–3 seconds for the chart to load real market data.
03
⚙️ Configure Your Settings

Set the Prediction Period using the slider — choose between 7 to 30 days ahead. Select your Historical Window (60, 90, or 180 days) and choose a Model Type (EMA Crossover, EMA + MACD, or Hybrid).

💡 For beginners, use the default EMA Crossover model with a 7-day prediction.
04
▶️ Run the Prediction

Click the green Run Prediction button. The EMA engine will compute EMA-12 and EMA-26, detect any crossover, and generate your forecast within seconds.

💡 The button shows a loading spinner while computing — wait for it to finish.
05
📊 Read the EMA Chart

The main chart shows three lines — the Price (green), EMA-12 (blue dashed), and EMA-26 (orange dashed). When EMA-12 crosses above EMA-26, a Golden Cross is formed. When it crosses below, a Death Cross is detected.

💡 Golden Cross = potential uptrend. Death Cross = potential downtrend.
06
🔮 View the Forecast

Scroll down to see the Predicted Trend chart and the Day-Wise Forecast Table. Each row shows the estimated price and a ▲/▼ signal badge for that day. The confidence score is shown in the signal banner.

💡 Higher confidence % = stronger signal strength.
07
📥 Use the Insights

Use the EMA signal alongside your own research to make an informed investment decision. Remember — StockSense provides data-driven trend signals, not guaranteed predictions. Always verify with additional sources.

📖 Quick Glossary
EMA Exponential Moving Average — gives more weight to recent prices.
EMA-12 Fast EMA — reacts quickly to recent price changes.
EMA-26 Slow EMA — smoother, longer-term trend indicator.
Golden Cross EMA-12 crosses above EMA-26 → Bullish (Uptrend) signal.
Death Cross EMA-12 crosses below EMA-26 → Bearish (Downtrend) signal.
OHLCV Open, High, Low, Close, Volume — standard stock price data.
